How we met

We met each other in Pittsburgh, PA through a co-worker, Melissa, when she set us up on a double date. We were both intensive care unit (ICU) nurses on the same unit but didn’t know each other very well. After several dates, we realized we both loved to explore new places so we decided to start travel nursing in Maryland. After a year of travel nursing, we both got accepted into CRNA school in Pennsylvania and Kansas. Even though we were 1000+ miles apart, our love continued to grow and we got engaged during school in Annapolis, MD on a boat! We are so happy to finally be done with the long distance relationship and start this new chapter of life!
